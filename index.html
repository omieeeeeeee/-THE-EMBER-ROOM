Our story

Started with a single
roaster and a dream

What began as a 200-sq-ft roastery near Upvan Lake in 2019 has grown into Thane's go-to spot for specialty coffee. We source directly from estates in Chikmagalur and Coorg, roasting every batch by hand in our open kitchen.

The Ember Room isn't just a café — it's where Thane's creatives, remote workers, and coffee lovers come to slow down. No rush, no noise, just the sound of beans cracking and milk steaming.
